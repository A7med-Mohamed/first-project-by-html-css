SERVICES OF DR-Food

Restaurant services can vary depending on the type and level of the restaurant. Some common services offered by restaurants include:

1- Reservations: Customers can reserve a table in advance to ensure availability upon arrival.

2- Table service: Customers sit at a table and food and drinks are served to them by a waiter or waitress.

3- Buffet service: Food is served at a specific location and customers can choose the dishes they want to eat.

4- Delivery service: Some restaurants offer delivery service for customers who want to get their food at their homes or offices.

5- Reception and welcome service: Customers are greeted with a smile and welcomed to the restaurant, and are directed to their seats.
